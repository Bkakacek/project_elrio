El Rio - A Mexican Restaurant

We were formed in 1989 as just a taco stand. After a few successful years, we made the transition of moving into the building we use today. We currently do not have any plans to expand to other towns, but we would like to continue to update our current restaurant, so it is up to date on the latest technology.

- We are the area's leading Mexican Restaurant.

- We serve our local area authentic Mexican food in a variety of ways.

- We offer outdoor seating with frequent live musical performances, dine-in, takeout, and more.

- Visit us for all your Mexican restaurant needs!
